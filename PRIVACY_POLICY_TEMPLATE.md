# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [Insert Date]

## 1. Introduction

Cyberstition ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Cyberstition mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

**Cyberstition does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data.**

We are committed to your privacy. Our App is designed to work entirely on your device without collecting or transmitting any data to external servers.

## 3. Local Processing Only

All scam detection, email analysis, profile verification, and image inspection happens entirely on your device. This includes:

- Message content analysis
- Email header inspection
- Social profile verification
- Image metadata extraction
- Pattern detection and risk assessment

**No data leaves your device during any analysis process.**

## 4. Local Storage

Analysis results and scan history are stored locally on your device only. This data includes:

- Scan session history
- Analysis reports
- User preferences
- App settings

You can delete this data at any time through the app's settings or by uninstalling the app.

## 5. In-App Purchases

We use Google Play Billing for in-app purchases. When you make a purchase:

- Purchase transactions are processed by Google Play
- We receive purchase verification information from Google
- We do not have access to your payment information
- Google handles all payment processing according to Google's privacy policy

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 6. Analytics and Tracking

**We do not use any analytics services, tracking tools, or advertising networks.**

The App does not:
- Track your usage
- Collect analytics data
- Use advertising identifiers
- Share data with third parties

## 7. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party service:

**Google Play Billing**
- Purpose: Processing in-app purchases
- Data: Purchase verification tokens (handled by Google)
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

No other third-party services are integrated into the App.

## 8. Internet Connection

The App can function offline for most features. An internet connection may be required for:
- Initial app download and updates
- In-app purchase verification
- Accessing Google Play services

No user data is transmitted during these operations.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

## 10. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store your data on external servers, there is no risk of data breaches from our servers. All data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's security measures.

## 11. Your Rights

Since we do not collect your data, there is no data for us to:
- Access
- Modify
- Delete
- Export

All data stored locally on your device can be deleted by:
- Clearing app data in device settings
- Uninstalling the app

## 12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Notifying you through the App (if significant changes occur)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## 13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

**Email:** support@ermits.com

**Response Time:** We aim to respond within 24-48 hours.

## 14. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
- Google Play Store policies

## 15. Consent

By using our App, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.
