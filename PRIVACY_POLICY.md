# Privacy Policy for Cyberstition

**Last Updated:** February 16, 2026

## Introduction

Cyberstition ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Analysis Data**: When you use our scam detection features, you may provide messages, emails, images, profiles, or videos for analysis. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any external services.

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: We do not collect usage analytics or tracking data.
- **Device Information**: We do not collect device identifiers or personal device information.
- **Location Data**: We do not collect location information.

## How We Use Information

### On-Device Processing
All analysis and processing of your data occurs entirely on your device. We do not:
- Store your analysis data on our servers
- Transmit your data over the internet
- Share your data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising or marketing

### Local Storage
- Analysis results are stored locally on your device
- You can export or delete this data at any time through the app
- Data is stored using standard device storage mechanisms

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to third parties. Your data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

## External Services

While this app operates entirely on your device, we may offer separate web-based services (such as advanced deepfake detection) that require data upload. These services:
- Are completely optional and separate from this app
- Have their own privacy policies
- Require explicit action to use (visiting a different website)
- Are clearly labeled as external services

This app itself does not connect to or share data with any external services.

## In-App Purchases

If you make in-app purchases:
- Purchase transactions are processed by Google Play Store
- We receive purchase verification data (not personal information)
- Payment information is handled by Google, not by us
- See Google's Privacy Policy for payment processing details

## Data Security

- All processing occurs on your device
- No data transmission over networks
- Standard device security measures apply
- You control all data through the app's export/delete features

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored in the app
- **Export**: Export your data as JSON or PDF
- **Delete**: Delete any or all data at any time
- **Control**: All data remains under your control

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- **Email**: support@ermits.com

## Data Processing Summary

- **Data Collection**: Minimal (only what you provide for analysis)
- **Data Storage**: Local device only
- **Data Transmission**: None
- **Data Sharing**: None
- **Third-Party Services**: Google Play Store (for purchases only)
- **Analytics**: None
- **Advertising**: None

---

**Your Privacy Matters**: Cyberstition is designed with privacy as a core principle. All analysis happens on your device, and your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.
